MFFS Identification Card are used in combination with the Biometric Identifier to govern the rights and privileges of
players.

## Usage

Before an Identification Card can be used, it needs to be assigned to a player. An Identification Card can be assigned
to yourself by shift-right-clicking while holding the card. You can also shift-right-click other players to assign the
card to them. Activate the card again to clear the stored identity.
