id: mffs:speed_module
type: item
---

The Speed Module can be placed in the upgrade slots of various MFFS machines to upgrade their performance.

Its effects vary depending on the machine, but most commonly include:

- Speeding up force field projection
- Increasing the Fortron transfer limit of a Fortron Capacitor
- Increasing the efficiency of a Coercion Deriver
