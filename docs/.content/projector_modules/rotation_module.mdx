# Rotation Module

The Rotation Module can be placed in the field slots of the [](@mffs:projector) to rotate the projected field.

You can move the force field around the x, y or z axis by placing Rotation Modules in the respective slot for that axis.

- EAST, WEST - Adjust the Yaw
- UP, DOWN - Adjust the Pitch
- SOUTH, NORTH - Adjust the Roll
